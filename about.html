WHO WE ARE

Suthern Studios is a solo indie game development studio building deep, systems-driven games with handcrafted pixel art. We believe in tight mechanics, interconnected systems, and worlds that feel alive — not polished to perfection, but crafted with purpose.

> MISSION_

Build games with soul. No filler, no fluff — deep systems, meaningful choices, and worlds worth surviving in.

> STRUCTURE_

One person. All code, all design, all art direction. Built solo from the ground up — from world generation to combat AI.

> GENRE.FOCUS_

Colony sims, survival strategy, base builders, and procedurally generated worlds — if every system connects, we're making it.

> STATUS_

First title AfterShock in active development. Coming to Steam Early Access.

PHILOSOPHY.LOG

> SYSTEMS OVER SPECTACLE

A game is only as good as the way its pieces talk to each other. We'd rather have ten deeply connected systems than a hundred shallow ones. What you grow feeds who you keep alive — that's not a tagline, it's an architecture decision.

> PIXEL ART IS A CHOICE, NOT A SHORTCUT

32×32 tiles rendered through URP 2D. Every sprite is deliberate. Pixel art isn't a budget compromise — it's a design philosophy that forces clarity and intention into every visual element.

> BUILD IN PUBLIC

No NDAs, no smoke and mirrors. Development happens in the open. When something breaks, you'll hear about it. When something clicks, you'll see it first.

> MODDABLE BY DEFAULT

Games belong to their communities. AfterShock is built with mod-friendly architecture and full modding rights from day one. If you can dream it, you should be able to build it.
