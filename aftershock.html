README.TXT

AfterShock is a deep colony simulation set in the aftermath of civilization's collapse. Every world is procedurally generated from a single seed — layering terrain, climate, biomes, radiation zones, ruined cities, and road networks across a full world map before you ever place your first wall.

Your colonists are more than numbers. They get hungry, exhausted, and stressed. Their mood drops when needs go unmet, and pushed too far, they break. Assign jobs, prioritize work, build defenses, and craft gear through a flexible recipe and production system. When raiders come, your colonists fight back with melee and ranged combat, using cover and tactical AI.

Explore the wasteland on expeditions, farm crops, raise animals, mine deep deposits, and smelt ingots. Every system connects — what you grow feeds who you keep alive.
